Our Project

We wanted to give back to the Open Source Community and thought that the idea of an unconventional mirror was really cool. After hours of binge watching Youtube Videos for ideas, we came across the Magic Mirror Open Source Platform. Now the question is, how do you make this better ?

We Decided To Add Some Artificial Intelligence

  • Your Mirror Recognizes You and talks accordingly.
  • Take A Selfie
  • Ask Your Mirror For a Joke
  • Pull Up Your Facebook Feed
  • We Trained Our Mirror to Hold Complex & Meaningful Conversations
  • Play a wide range of Motivational Videos to get you Going in the morning
  • Stay Updated on Traffic, News, and the Weather

    Face++ API

    Google Maps API
